About Us

Our mission to glorify God by raising people and rebuilding communities

Nashery's Global Initiative (NGI) is a faith-based, nonprofit organization dedicated to transforming lives through compassion, faith, and purpose. We focus on empowering orphans, the less privileged, and vulnerable individuals through strategic outreaches, volunteer mobilization, and spiritual training.

Though many of our planned institutions are still in development, NGI is already actively impacting lives across Nigeria through our yearly youth conference (ZGIYC), street outreaches, and grassroots mobilization.

We exist to glorify God by raising people, rebuilding communities, and reshaping destinies.

Our long-term vision includes establishing a center that will host our conference, educational, healthcare, welfare, and entrepreneurial training programs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our organization

What is NGI?

Nashery's Global Initiative (NGI) is a interdenominational organization. We welcome volunteers and participants from all Christian denominations who share our mission to serve communities in need.

How can I become a volunteer?

You can apply to become a volunteer by completing our online application form. After submission, our volunteer coordinator will contact you for an orientation session. We require all volunteers to complete basic training before participating in field activities.

How are donations utilized?

90% of all donations go directly to program activities. 10% supports administrative and operational costs. We publish annual financial reports for full transparency.

Do you partner with other organizations?

Yes, we actively seek partnerships with churches, faith-based organizations, and community groups. Our current partners include the Collen Mashawana Foundation and several local churches across Nigeria.

How can I stay updated on NGI's activities?

Subscribe to our newsletter, follow us on social media, or join our Telegram group for regular updates. All our communication channels are listed in the contact section.
