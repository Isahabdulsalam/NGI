About Us

Our mission to glorify God by raising people and rebuilding communities

Nashery's Global Initiative (NGI) is a faith-based, nonprofit organization dedicated to transforming lives through compassion, faith, and purpose. We focus on empowering orphans, the less privileged, and vulnerable individuals through strategic outreaches, volunteer mobilization, and spiritual training.

Though many of our planned institutions are still in development, NGI is already actively impacting lives across Nigeria through our yearly youth conference (ZGIYC), street outreaches, and grassroots mobilization.

We exist to glorify God by raising people, rebuilding communities, and reshaping destinies.

Our long-term vision includes establishing a center that will host our conference, educational, healthcare, welfare, and entrepreneurial training programs.

What We Do

Our current initiatives and future vision for transforming communities

ZGI Youth Conference

Our annual flagship gathering of young people across denominations for spiritual growth, training, and purpose discovery.

Community Outreaches

Spiritual and humanitarian outreaches to schools, streets, orphanages, and communities, providing food, counsel, and support.

Volunteer Mobilization

Coordinating faith-driven action through our fast-growing national network of volunteers to reach the unreached.

Our Vision for the Future

We're building a comprehensive ecosystem of faith-based services to transform communities across Nigeria.

NGI Conference Center

A permanent facility for spiritual gatherings and training events.

NGI Orphanage & Welfare Home

Safe haven and holistic care for orphans and vulnerable children.

Nashery Nation Academy

Quality education with a focus on character and leadership development.

NGI Medical Center

Healthcare services for underserved communities.

Why Nashery's Global Initiative

Our purpose and mission for transformation

Because lives must be transformed, and God must be glorified. NGI was founded to be a solution; a bridge between compassion and impact.

Our Mission:

  • To reach people physically and spiritually
  • To raise leaders through faith and service
  • To inspire lasting hope in communities
  • To unite volunteers and believers for national transformation

We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to live a life of purpose, dignity, and destiny.
